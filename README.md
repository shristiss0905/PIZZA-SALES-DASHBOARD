### **Title**: Comprehensive Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard  

**Description**:  
This repository features a detailed Power BI dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales data. The dashboard leverages interactive visualizations and key metrics to help businesses understand customer preferences, optimize sales strategies, and track overall performance.

### Key Features:
- **High-Level KPIs**:  
  - **Total Revenue**: $817.86K  
  - **Total Quantity Sold**: 50K units  
  - **Total Orders Placed**: 21.35K orders
    
  These figures give a snapshot of the overall business performance during the analysis period.

- **Interactive Date Filters**:  
  A date range filter (1/1/2015 - 12/12/2015) allows users to analyze data for specific periods, enabling time-based insights and seasonal trend evaluation.

- **Category Insights**:  
  Visualizations of pizza sales segmented by categories (Chicken, Classic, Supreme, Veggie) to identify customer preferences and top-performing product lines.

- **Sales by Size**:  
  Analyze sales distribution across pizza sizes (L, M, S, XL, XXL) with detailed metrics, including the percentage contribution of each size to total sales.

- **Hourly Sales Trends**:  
  An hourly trend graph highlights peak order times, providing insights into customer ordering behavior and optimal staffing or promotion periods.

- **Daily Sales Patterns**:  
  Breakdown of sales quantities and revenue by day, identifying consistent performers and anomalies across the time range.

- **Top Categories and Popular Items**:  
  Identify the top-selling pizza categories and understand their contribution to total sales, aiding in inventory and marketing optimization.

- **Drill-Down Capabilities**:  
  Users can filter data by pizza names, sizes, and categories for a detailed, granular analysis, enabling actionable insights for decision-making.

### Business Applications:
- **Strategic Planning**: Use data-driven insights to optimize inventory, staffing, and marketing strategies.  
- **Customer Behavior Analysis**: Understand customer preferences by category, size, and time to tailor promotions and menu offerings.  
- **Sales Trend Monitoring**: Identify growth opportunities and underperforming areas with detailed trend analysis.  

### How to Use:
1. Download the `PBI5.pbix` file from this repository.  
2. Open it in Power BI Desktop (or publish it to the Power BI service).  
3. Use the interactive dashboard features to explore and analyze pizza sales data.  
4. Customize the filters and visualizations to meet specific business needs.

This dashboard serves as a powerful tool for pizza businesses aiming to gain actionable insights into their sales data and make informed decisions to enhance profitability and customer satisfaction.
